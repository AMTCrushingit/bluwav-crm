Security updates, content edits, performance monitoring, uptime checks, and technical support. Your website stays fast, secure, and up to date every month.
Do I really own my website forever?
Yes. Every plan includes a signed agreement confirming you own your website. If you ever leave, you take your website with you — no questions asked.
What's the difference between CRM Starter ($39) and Premium ($79)?
CRM Starter gives you the core features for growing businesses. CRM Premium includes every future upgrade, advanced automation, advanced reporting, and priority support — everything we build going forward is included.
Can I upgrade from CRM Starter to Premium later?
Yes, anytime. You'll pay the difference in monthly fee. The setup fee is already waived if you upgraded from your trial.
Is the 30-day CRM trial really free?
Yes — no credit card required. Every BluWav Growth plan includes a full 30-day trial of CRM Lite so you can experience the platform before choosing your CRM plan.
How quickly will my website be delivered?
Most websites are delivered within 48–72 hours of receiving your content and approval. We move fast so you can start growing fast.
